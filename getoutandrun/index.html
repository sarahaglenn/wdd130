Welcome to Get Out And Run Coaching!

At Get Out And Run, we help athletes of all ages become their healthiest, fastest selves. We believe in prioritizing rest days, utilizing valuable workouts, and fueling for success. Get to know our coaches below.

Coach Sarah

I have been a lover of running since middle school cross country. I've run numerous 5Ks and 2-milers, a few 10Ks, and three half marathons. It's tough to pick a favorite distance. I started coaching in 2017 when a friend asked me to build a training plan for her to run her first 5K. I couldn't help but stay involved in the process beyond writing the plan. I loved getting to watch her develop a love and appreciation for running, and I feel confident that I can help you do the same.

Coach Andrew

I started running when I spontaneously signed up for my high school's track team. At the end of the season, my coach convinced me to try cross country. I loved cross country even more, and went on to run cross country all through college. I was an NCAA champion and quickly realized that I wanted to spread the love of running with as many people as possible. I started coaching friends and family after college and have continually expanded my clients since then. I love being able to help people achieve their goals and accomplish even more than they thought possible.

"Running allows me to set my mind free. Nothing seems impossible. Nothing unattainable."
