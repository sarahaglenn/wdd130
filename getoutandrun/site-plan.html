Meet the Coaches

Coach Sarah!
I have been a lover of running since middle school cross country. I’ve run numerous 5Ks and 2-milers, a few 10Ks, and 3 half marathons. It’s tough to pick a favorite distance. I started coaching in 2017 when a friend asked me to build a training plan for them to run their first 5K. I couldn’t help but stay involved in the process beyond writing the plan. I loved getting to watch her develop a love and appreciation for running and I feel confident that I can help you do the same.
Coach Andrew!

I started running by signing up for my high school’s track team until my coach convinced me to try cross country. I loved cross country even more, and went on to run cross country all through college. I was an NCAA champion my senior year and quickly realized that I wanted to spread the love of running with as many people as possible. I started coaching friends and family after college and have continually expanded my clients since then. I love people able to help people achieve their goals and achieve even more than they thought was possible.

Why should you work with Get Out and Run?
Support: Having someone in your corner when you embark on a new goal is a huge contributor to later success. Let us take the guesswork out of the planning and be here to support, encourage, and guide you along the way to achieving your next running success story. Three years after starting out, I’m running farther than I ever pictured I would and feel strong and healthy to show up for my family when I get back from my run. -Sammy
